Weather App

We experience several effects of the weather. Weather variations from day to day can affect how we feel and how we perceive the world. The transient conditions of the atmosphere, the layer of air that surrounds the Earth, are referred to as "weather." The atmosphere in our own region of the world is typically how we conceive of weather. However, the way that weather operates is similar to how dropping a pebble in water; the ripples eventually affect water far from the original location. The weather occurs similarly everywhere around the world. The weather hundreds or thousands of kilometers away will eventually be impacted by the weather in your area. Click on "learn more" to see the current weather condition.
